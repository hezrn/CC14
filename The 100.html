The 100

A deeper dive into the world of The 100 series.

This series is one of my absolute favorite because it’s a perfect mix of action, survival, and emotional storytelling. The twists and turns, the battles for survival, and the relationships between the characters make it unforgettable. The character development is amazing, and it always keeps me on the edge of my seat with its twists and turns.

Summary:

The 100 is a post-apocalyptic sci-fi series that follows a group of 100 juvenile delinquents sent to Earth after humanity's self-destruction.

Season 1: the group discovers that Earth is habitable again and faces dangers from the environment and the descendants of survivors, known as the Grounders.

Season 2: they struggle with leadership conflicts and a new enemy, the Mountain Men, while uncovering more about the past.

Season 3: sees the rise of a new threat, the AI A.L.I.E., who tries to take control of humanity's future.

Season 4: the group deals with a nuclear apocalypse that threatens to end all life.

Season 5: focuses on survival in space after the nuclear disaster, as well as a new war on Earth involving a group known as the Eligius prisoners.

Season 6: the survivors arrive on a new planet, Sanctum, and must navigate new political and survival challenges.

Season 7: the final season, explores the remnants of humanity’s hope for a new future, with the group fighting against an existential threat while reflecting on their journey and the cost of survival.

Key Themes:

Survival, Strategy, Leadership, Sacrifice, Family, Power Struggles.

My Favorite Characters:

  • Clarke Griffin: A natural leader and the central character, determined to protect her people.
  • Bellamy Blake: A strong-willed leader who shares Clarke’s commitment to the survival of the group.
  • Raven Reyes: A brilliant mechanic and engineer who helps keep the group’s technology running.
  • Octavia Blake: Bellamy’s sister, who undergoes significant character growth throughout the series.

The Originals

An in-depth look at the timeless drama of The Originals.

As a spin-off of The Vampire Diaries, The Originals had big shoes to fill, and it absolutely delivered! It’s darker and more intense, focusing on the powerful Mikaelson family. I love how it dives into the history of the vampires and explores themes like family loyalty and betrayal. I love most is how it explores themes of power and family, showing that even the strongest have their weaknesses. The New Orleans setting with all its supernatural vibes just adds even more magic to the show.

Summary:

The Originals delves into the lives of the Mikaelson family, the original vampires, as they return to New Orleans to reclaim their legacy. The series explores family bonds, power struggles, betrayal, and the unrelenting love that holds them together.

Season 1: The Mikaelsons return to New Orleans to reclaim the city from Marcel. Klaus discovers Hayley is pregnant, introducing a new dynamic with his unborn daughter, Hope. Witches, vampires, and werewolves vie for power, leading to alliances and betrayals. The season ends with the birth of Hope and a brutal witch attack that forces Klaus to fake Hope's death and send her away for safety.

Season 2: The return of the Mikaelsons’ parents, Esther and Mikael, along with long-lost siblings Finn and Kol, creates chaos. Esther tries to "redeem" her children by erasing their vampirism. The season explores family tension, Klaus’s vulnerability, and Hope's emerging powers. Hope is brought back to New Orleans, solidifying her role in the family.

Season 3: A prophecy predicts the Mikaelsons' downfall, driving them to protect themselves. New enemies emerge, including the Strix, an ancient vampire society tied to Elijah. The season sees heightened tensions between family members and allies as the prophecy plays out. Marcel becomes a major threat after taking a serum that makes him more powerful than the Mikaelsons.

Season 4: After a five-year time jump, the Mikaelsons are resurrected to face a new enemy: The Hollow, a malevolent spirit targeting Hope. The family bands together, making painful sacrifices to protect Hope and defeat The Hollow. Klaus separates himself from Hope to ensure her safety.

Season 5: The final season focuses on Hope as a teenager grappling with her powers and the consequences of her family’s actions. The Hollow resurfaces, threatening Hope’s life. Klaus and Elijah make the ultimate sacrifice to destroy The Hollow and save their family. The series ends with Klaus and Elijah finding peace together in the afterlife, and Hope preparing to continue their legacy.

Key Themes:

Family, Loyalty, Betrayal, Power, Legacy, and Redemption.

My Favorite Characters:

  • Klaus Mikaelson: The hybrid of vampire and werewolf, ambitious and fiercely protective of his family.
  • Hope Mikaelson: Klaus and Hayley's daughter, born with extraordinary powers as the first tribrid (witch, werewolf, and vampire).
  • Hayley Marshall: A werewolf and Klaus's love interest, fiercely determined to protect her child.
